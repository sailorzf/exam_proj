\## Brand \& Style



The design system is anchored in a \*\*Modern Minimalist\*\* aesthetic, tailored specifically for high-stakes digital environments. The brand personality is defined by four core pillars: \*\*Professionalism, Clarity, Focus, and Trust\*\*. 



To foster an environment conducive to deep concentration, the UI removes non-essential decorative elements, favoring generous whitespace and a rhythmic vertical cadence. The visual style utilizes subtle depth through soft elevation rather than flat abstraction, ensuring that interactive elements are intuitively discoverable without causing cognitive fatigue. The result is a utilitarian yet sophisticated interface that prioritizes the user's mental bandwidth during examination and administrative tasks.



\## Colors



The color strategy uses \*\*Deep Indigo\*\* as the primary driver of action and brand recognition, chosen for its psychological associations with intelligence and stability. The \*\*Soft Sky Blue\*\* serves as a functional secondary color, primarily used for highlighting selections, progress indicators, or supportive informational banners without overwhelming the primary call to action.



The neutral palette is grounded in \*\*Very Light Gray\*\* for backgrounds to reduce screen glare, paired with \*\*Slate Gray\*\* for typography to ensure a sophisticated contrast ratio that exceeds WCAG 2.1 AA standards. Semantic colors for success, error, and warning states are calibrated to maintain high visibility against the neutral background.



\## Typography



The typography system leverages \*\*Inter\*\* for its exceptional legibility in digital interfaces and its neutral, systematic character. 



A strict hierarchy is maintained to guide the user through complex question structures. \*\*Body-lg\*\* is specifically designated for examination questions, utilizing a generous line-height of 1.7 to prevent line-tracking errors during long-form reading. Headlines use a tighter tracking and heavier weight to provide clear structural anchoring on dashboards and management views. Labels and captions utilize a medium weight to ensure legibility at smaller scales.



\## Layout \& Spacing



This design system employs a \*\*Fixed Grid\*\* approach for internal content containers to ensure optimal line lengths for reading, while utilizing a \*\*Fluid Grid\*\* for the overarching dashboard structure. 



A 12-column grid system is used for management tables and dashboard layouts, with a standard 24px gutter to maintain visual separation. Spacing follows a 4px baseline rhythm, ensuring all margins and paddings are multiples of 4. Whitespace is used aggressively between question blocks to provide "breathing room," reducing the perceived complexity of the examination process.



\## Elevation \& Depth



Visual hierarchy is established through \*\*Ambient Shadows\*\* and \*\*Tonal Layers\*\*. This design system avoids heavy gradients, opting instead for extra-diffused, low-opacity shadows (e.g., `0 4px 6px -1px rgba(30, 41, 59, 0.1)`) to lift interactive components like cards and primary buttons.



Depth levels are defined as:

\- \*\*Level 0 (Base):\*\* Background (#F8FAFC), used for the main canvas.

\- \*\*Level 1 (Surface):\*\* Pure white (#FFFFFF) with a thin 1px border (#E2E8F0), used for question cards and content modules.

\- \*\*Level 2 (Elevated):\*\* White surfaces with soft shadows, used for active states, modals, and floating action panels.



\## Shapes



The shape language is consistently \*\*Rounded\*\*, utilizing a 0.5rem (8px) base radius for standard components like input fields and buttons. Larger containers, such as question cards and dashboard widgets, utilize the `rounded-lg` (16px) or `rounded-xl` (24px) tokens to soften the overall interface and evoke a modern, approachable feel. This consistent rounding helps distinguish the modern web application from legacy institutional software.



\## Components



\- \*\*Buttons:\*\* Primary buttons use a solid Deep Indigo fill with white text. Secondary buttons use a transparent background with an Indigo border. CTAs must have a minimum height of 44px to ensure touch-target compliance.

\- \*\*Input Fields:\*\* Use a white background, 1px Slate Gray border (30% opacity), and 8px rounded corners. Active states transition the border to Deep Indigo with a soft outer glow.

\- \*\*Question Cards:\*\* High-contrast containers with 24px internal padding. They should feature a "Focus Mode" where only the active card is fully opaque, and others are slightly dimmed.

\- \*\*Progress Indicators:\*\* Use Soft Sky Blue for the track and Deep Indigo for the progress fill, providing a clear but non-distracting visual cue.

\- \*\*Data Tables:\*\* Clean, borderless rows with subtle dividers (#E2E8F0). The header should be sticky with a slight elevation when scrolling.

\- \*\*Status Chips:\*\* Small, 100px rounded badges using 10% opacity fills of the semantic colors (Success, Error, Warning) with high-contrast text labels.

\- \*\*Timer/Countdown:\*\* Fixed-position element in the top-right, utilizing a monospaced variant of Inter for numerical stability.
